About Me

Hi, I’m Arnav Nair, a quantitative researcher passionate about developing systematic trading strategies and applying advanced statistical methods to financial markets. My work spans from Kalman filtering and Markov Chains to options backtesting and statistical arbitrage. I enjoy bridging the gap between theory and implementation, with an emphasis on risk management and real-world applicability.

Statistical Arbitrage with Kalman Filtering

Developed a pairs trading strategy using a Kalman filter to dynamically estimate the spread and hedge ratio between two stocks. Backtested the strategy with ~60% win rate, and implemented hedging paramters to limit losses within the arbitrage framework.

Markov Chain Market Regime Strategy (In Progress)

Constructed a Markov Chain model to capture transition probabilities between up and down market days. Strategy identifies recurring patterns for directional trading while layering in hedging strategies.
