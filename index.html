US Health Volatility Surface

Modeled US obesity prevalence as a latent underlying on the logit scale and constructed a synthetic implied uncertainty surface by calibrating crossing probabilities of obesity thresholds (25%–45%) across horizons (1–10y) using CDC BRFSS data. The background wireframe visualizes this real (T, K) → σ surface — a quant lens on public health risk.
